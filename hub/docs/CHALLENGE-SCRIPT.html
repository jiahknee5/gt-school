2 · ASSESS

On screen

the result screen — the child's fit bucket

Talking points

highIt's a fit screen, not a pass/fail on a kid. There's no "not gifted" bucket.

techA deterministic AI grader behind a swappable interface, with a rules floor so capture never blocks on the model. It buckets strong-fit / promising / explore; "qualified" is what drives routing and the cost-per-qualified-lead.
