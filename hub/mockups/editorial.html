Executive Narrative

Editable weekly · the Marketing Lead
Topline

847 applicants on the board — up 63 week-over-week — and 96 deposits against the 180 Fall goal. We are pacing to roughly 168 by Aug 17, just shy. The story isn’t volume; it’s that the warm channels are doing the heavy lifting and we should feed them.

What’s working

X is the 42% engine. Clicked-tier families commit at 52% versus 16% cold — engagement is still our single best predictor. Grassroots ambassadors are warming faster than expected and K–2 remains the sweet spot.

What’s stuck

UTM attribution is broken and now blocking per-piece content credit. Facebook keeps posting volume that doesn’t deposit. Grassroots spend has tipped 11% over plan — already auto-flagged to the queue.

Decisions needed
  • 1Reallocate $18K from Facebook into the X / Substack engine.
  • 2Approve the July guerrilla / earned-media bet ($40K line).
  • 3Greenlight the UTM rebuild sprint before the August push.
